How Does This Work?

1. Create a Platformer Game using Godot

Godot is a free game engine you can use to create games.

Once you download Godot, setup Godot Hackatime before you start building your game. Follow this guide. (VERY IMPORTANT!)

Then, follow along with the 10-minute speedrun tutorial below or use the written guide to create a base 2D platformer game.

2. Make Your Game Unique

Choose a theme/style for your platformer. Add new features to make your game better! You can add your own story, background, assets, and more. For inspiration, look at the some of the other platformer games made by Hack Clubbers with Godot below!

Signal Lost

A space platformer with gravity changing and dangerous plants.

Made by Estella Gu, you can play it at here!

Cocytus

A cave platformer where you move with your mouse.

Made by Sarah Ngai, you can play it at here!

Echoes

An adventure platformer where you move with a lantern.

Made by Olive Wu, you can play it at here!

PlatformLand

A land platformer where you explore ancient ruins and avoid dangerous biomes.

Made by Julia Nadolska, you can play it at here!

3. Publish your Project to Itch.io

Itch.io is a website for people to share and play video games. Make an account, export your game, and publish it to the world. Use this video to help you.

4. Ship your Project (CONGRATS!!)

Ship your project in the #jumpstart-daydream channel in the format below.

Then submit your game the form on the right!

5. Run a Jumpstart workshop at your Daydream, get stickers!

Now that you're experienced, you can run a Jumpstart workshop at your Daydream and we'll ship you the stickersheets below for your event.

Make sure to indicate that you are going to run this in the form and I'll be reaching out with more support and resources closer to the event.
